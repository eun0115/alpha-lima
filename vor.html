VOR Procedures

Master VOR navigation techniques. Expand lessons below for detailed procedures

  1. Twist OBS until TO indication with CDI centered.
  2. With CDI centered, head the course.
  3. If CDI deflects, re-center the CDI.
  4. Fly the homing heading until reaching the station.
  1. Twist OBS to center CDI.
  2. Check TO-FROM indication.
  3. If TO indication, the reciprocal course index is the LOP.
  4. If FROM indication, the course index is the LOP.
  1. Determine the radial to be intercepted by checking the LOP and desired radial.
  2. Twist proper OBS, set the desired radial to the reciprocal course index and confirm if TO indication.
  3. Intercept radial by using 30°, 60° and 90° of your course. ("+" right CDI deflection, "-" if left)
  4. CDI active, fly the course.
  1. Determine the radial to be intercepted by checking the LOP and desired radial.
  2. Twist proper OBS, set the desired radial to the course index and confirm if FROM indication.
  3. Intercept radial by using 30°, 45° and 60° of your course. ("+" right CDI deflection, "-" if left)
  4. CDI active, fly the course.
  1. Determine the radial to be intercepted.
  2. Twist proper OBS and confirm FROM.
  3. Fly parallel and wait for change flag.
  4. Change flag, time for 2 minutes.
  5. End time, Proceed to basic interception.
  1. Determine the radial to be intercepted.
  2. Twist proper OBS and confirm TO.
  3. Fly parallel and wait for change flag.
  4. Proceed to basic interception.
  1. Head 45° left/right of present course.
  2. On the heading, time for 45 second.
  3. Twist OBS to the reciprocal of present course.
  4. End time, turn opposite of initial turn and interceptradial.

Commence at 10% of your airspeed + DME to hold
(10% GS + DME)

  1. Twist OBS -10° of present course.
  2. Head +90° of the course.
  3. Center CDI, twist OBS -10°.
  4. Turn -10° of heading.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until intercept inbound ofdesired radial.

  4. Turn +10° of heading.

  5. CDI center, end time.
  6. Home to station.
